## OCR Engines

### Tesseract
> Smith, R. (2007). An overview of the Tesseract OCR engine. *In Ninth International Conference on Document Analysis and Recognition (ICDAR 2007)* (Vol. 2, pp. 629-633). IEEE.

### EasyOCR
> JaidedAI. (2020). EasyOCR: Ready-to-use OCR with 80+ Languages Supported. https://github.com/JaidedAI/EasyOCR

### TrOCR (Planned)
> Li, M., Chen, Y., Yu, W., Shi, S., & Liu, G. (2021). TrOCR: Transformer-based Optical Character Recognition with Pre-trained Models. *arXiv preprint arXiv:2109.10282*.
