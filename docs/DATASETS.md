# OmniOCR – Supported Datasets

This document lists sample and benchmark datasets that OmniOCR supports or recommends for evaluation and training.

---

## 🧪 Public Datasets for Evaluation

### Persian OCR
- **Persian_OCR**
  - Source: https://github.com/mohammadpz/Persian_OCR
  - Content: Printed Persian text in scanned images
  - Format: JPG + TXT

- **FarsiHandwriting**
  - Source: https://github.com/amir-saniyan/FarsiHandwriting
  - Content: Handwritten Persian words
  - Format: PNG + CSV

---

## 🌍 Multilingual OCR

- **IIIT-5K Words Dataset**
  - Source: https://cvit.iiit.ac.in/research/projects/cvit-projects/the-iiit-5k-word-dataset
  - Format: Cropped word images

- **SynthText**
  - Source: https://github.com/ankush-me/SynthText
  - Content: Synthetic scene text in English
  - Format: Image + annotation

---

## 📊 Dataset Usage in OmniOCR
- All datasets can be used for:
  - Performance benchmarking
  - Postprocessing evaluation
  - Fine-tuning language models
